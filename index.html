Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Photoesque ("we," "our," or "us"). Photoesque is an iOS application developed by Zenith Labs that uses AI-powered photo analysis to help users select the best photo from a group. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access or use the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos

When you use Photoesque, you may select photos from your device library for analysis. These photos are:

  • Temporarily converted to base64 format for transmission
  • Sent to our secure backend server (hosted on Railway) via encrypted HTTPS connection
  • Forwarded to OpenAI's GPT-4o Vision API for AI-powered quality analysis
  • NOT stored on our servers after analysis is complete
  • NOT stored on OpenAI's servers after processing (per OpenAI's API terms)
  • Processed in real-time and immediately discarded

Analysis results (quality scores) are temporarily stored only in your device's memory and are not transmitted or stored elsewhere.

2.2 Subscription Data

Photoesque offers a subscription service priced at $1.99 per week with a 3-day free trial. Subscription management is handled through:

  • Superwall SDK: Used for paywall presentation and subscription management
  • Apple StoreKit: Handles all payment processing and transaction management

We do NOT store credit card information or payment details. All payment information is managed exclusively by Apple through StoreKit. Subscription status and transaction data are processed by Apple, and we only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to minimal data collection. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, or location
  • User behavior analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any data through cookies or tracking pixels
  • Photos permanently (they are processed and immediately discarded)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • Photo Analysis: To provide AI-powered quality analysis of your selected photos using OpenAI's GPT-4o Vision API
  • Service Delivery: To deliver the core functionality of the Photoesque application
  • Subscription Management: To manage your subscription status and provide access to premium features

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than providing the core functionality of the Photoesque application.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We share your information only with the following third-party services, and only to the extent necessary to provide Photoesque's functionality:

4.1 OpenAI API (GPT-4o Vision)

  • Purpose: Used solely for analyzing photo quality and providing AI-powered recommendations
  • Data Shared: Photos you select for analysis (temporarily, in base64 format)
  • Security: All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS
  • Retention: OpenAI does not retain photos after processing, per their API terms of service
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

4.2 Superwall

  • Purpose: Paywall presentation and subscription management
  • Data Shared: Subscription events and status information only
  • Privacy Policy: https://superwall.com/privacy

4.3 Apple StoreKit

  • Purpose: Payment processing and subscription transaction management
  • Data Shared: Subscription status and transaction confirmations
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit
  • Secure Storage: API keys and sensitive credentials are stored securely on our backend servers, not in the mobile application
  • Minimal Retention: We do not permanently store your photos or personal information
  • No Local Storage: Photos are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after analysis

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to implementing industry-standard security measures.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

6.1 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of any data we hold about you. However, please note that we do not permanently store your photos or personal information. Photos are processed and immediately discarded, and we do not maintain a database of user information.

6.2 Subscription Management

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your iOS device Settings:

  • Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
  • Select Photoesque
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription"

6.3 Photo Selection Control

You have complete control over which photos you select for analysis. Photoesque only processes photos that you explicitly choose to analyze.

6.4 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Information section below.

6.5 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Information section below.

7. Children's Privacy

Photoesque is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information from our systems.

If you are located in the EEA, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We do not permanently store your photos or personal information. Photos are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after analysis. Analysis results are stored only temporarily in your device's memory and are not retained on our servers.

Subscription-related data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the subscription service and comply with legal obligations.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. Specifically:

  • Photos may be processed by OpenAI's servers, which may be located outside your country
  • Our backend servers are hosted on Railway, which may process data in various locations

By using Photoesque, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
