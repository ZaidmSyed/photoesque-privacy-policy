Photoesque Support

AI-powered photo analysis to find your best shots. Photoesque uses advanced AI to analyze your photos based on sharpness, facial expressions, and lighting quality to help you choose the best photo from a burst.

How to Use Photoesque

  1. Select Photos: Choose photos taken in quick succession (within 1 minute of each other)
  2. Analyze: Tap the "Analyze" button to let AI rank your photos
  3. View Results: See your photos ranked by quality with detailed scores for sharpness, faces, and lighting
  4. Choose the Best: The top-ranked photo is your best shot!

Features

  • AI-Powered Analysis: Uses GPT-4o-mini vision AI to evaluate photo quality
  • Multiple Criteria: Analyzes sharpness, facial expressions, and lighting
  • Instant Results: Get ranked results in seconds
  • Privacy First: Photos are analyzed securely and never stored

Subscription

Photoesque offers a weekly subscription for unlimited photo analysis. You can manage your subscription through the Settings page in the app or through your Apple ID Subscriptions in the Settings app.
